**Description:** View your player profile including stats, rank, level, PALDOGS balance, and progress

**Usage:** `/balance`  
**What you'll see:** Your PALDOGS balance, total EXP, and current level

## üí∞ **Economy & Trading**

### `/give_paldogs [user] [amount]`
**Description:** Transfer PALDOGS to another player  
**Usage:** `/give_paldogs @username [amount]`  
**Examples:**
- `/give_paldogs @Friend 1000` - Send 1,000 PALDOGS to your friend
- `/give_paldogs @NewPlayer 500` - Help out a new player with 500 PALDOGS

**Important Notes:**
- You must have enough PALDOGS in your balance
- You cannot send PALDOGS to yourself
- Both players must have linked accounts

## üí° **Tips & Important Information**

### How to Earn PALDOGS:
- **Play on the server:** Automatic rewards for activities like crafting and building.
- **Oil Rig Raids:** Successfully raid Oil Rigs to earn rewards based on difficulty:
    - **Lv 60 Rig:** 15,000 PALDOGS
    - **Lv 55 Rig:** 10,000 PALDOGS
    - **Lv 30 Rig:** 5,000 PALDOGS
- **Combaticopter Bonus:** Defeat the helicopter at the Oil Rig for a massive **25,000 PALDOGS** bonus!
- **Events & Giveaways:** Participate in server events and giveaways.
- **Gifts:** Receive PALDOGS from other players using `/give_paldogs`.

### Account Linking:
- Your Discord account must be linked to your in-game character
- Admins can help you link your account if needed
- Once linked, you can use all player commands

### Claiming Rewards:
- Always be ONLINE in the server when claiming rewards from `/inventory`
- Rewards are delivered instantly via RCON to your in-game character
- If delivery fails, make sure you have inventory space

### Getting Help:
- Use `/palhelp` anytime to see the command list
- Contact server administrators if you encounter issues
- Check server announcements for updates and events
